Treona graduated with an honours degree in Music from the University of Huddersfield. She is a Music Director, vocal coach, piano tutor, professional accompanist, and the Musical Director for the Crewe Male Voice Choir. Treona collaborates with choirs, professional soloists, award-winning amateur dramatic societies, and youth theatre productions throughout Cheshire. Her impressive show credits include productions like Kinky Boots, Calendar Girls, Addams Family, Priscilla Queen of the Desert, Jesus Christ Superstar, My Fair Lady, The Wizard of Oz, A Chorus Line, and Seussical the Musical. Treona is also one of the founding directors of the professional theatre company, Platform Theatre. Additionally, she has had the privilege to perform with prestigious orchestras such as the Manchester Camerata, the Northern Chamber Orchestra, and the Liverpool Philharmonic Orchestra.
