We are always looking for new members!!

So, if you have a passion for music?

A love for singing?

Want to return to music after a long absence?

This may the choir for you!!!

First Steps...

Experience isn't important to us, but a basic level of ability is.
So before joining, we invite anyone interested to come along to a rehearsal (or 2) free of charge.
This is an opportunity for you to see if we are the right fit for you and vice versa.
The social aspect of the group is just as important to us as the performance, so it's important that both parties are happy :)

If you are interested, you can start by sending us a message through the CONTACT page above - we'll then be in touch to let you know when the next rehearsal you can attend will be.

What then?

After attending a rehearsal (or 2), a committee member will have a quick chat with you.

If the Musical Director (MD) is happy that they could hear you with the group and both parties are happy, you'll be invited to be a member.

If we were unable to hear you in the group - you may be invited to a very brief, informal one to one audition with the MD.
Don't worry, we're not looking for perfection, just that you can hold a tune and follow direction.
